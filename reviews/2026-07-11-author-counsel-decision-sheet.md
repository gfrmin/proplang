## R2 — rw / K (the molecule recorded as an atom)

**(a) What YES commits to.** `rw` is a DEBT, not printed residue: it decomposes into
`mix ρ (step) (id)` — three unpriced choices (nearest-neighbor topology, symmetric
split, reflecting boundary) folded into one codeword. Discharge at **K**: `rw` becomes
a stdlib name over individually priced, individually deletion-proven atoms; executed
path `walkOn` verbatim (anchors byte-stable, the bern precedent); demand-gated;
census-bearing; and K's census scopes the *now-generative* kernel-enumeration
frontier, with ≥1 **non-self-licensed** world making a newly-sayable molecule (e.g.
asymmetric drift) earn its bits.

**(b) What it forecloses.** Keeping `rw` as printed residue. The reviewer is right that
this is now the *worse* ruling: the audit itself exposed the decomposition, so printing
`rw` as an atom is knowingly printing a molecule — a documented content-injection.

**(c) Strongest case to reject/amend — the adjacency question, which the convergence
waved through.** The replacement derivation (`step` = *uniform over the neighbor set* —
interior points split ρ/2, an edge point's singleton neighbor set takes all ρ) claims
to derive topology, symmetry, and boundary "in one decision-free stroke." But **the
neighbor set presupposes an adjacency structure.** Uniform-over-neighbors is
decision-free only *after* you have fixed what "neighbor" means. The builder's position
is that adjacency comes free with the vocabulary the agent already owns. That is true —
**but only for an *ordered* carrier.** A finite totally-ordered grid (the Bernoulli
theta grid, ordered by log-odds) has exactly one canonical adjacency: the covering
relation of its order (successor/predecessor), forced the same way root-½ was forced by
symmetry. On an *unordered* carrier there is no canonical neighbor set — "neighbor"
would require declaring a graph, which is a smuggled choice, a *new* alphabet residue.
The reviewer's own "flips me" was "a demonstration that `step` cannot be stated
decision-free over the space's adjacency"; I am not showing it can't — I am showing its
freeness is **scoped to ordered carriers**, and K makes the KER sort *generative*, so
the frontier will eventually reach a carrier with no canonical order.

**(d) Recommendation.** **Ratify the DEBT verdict and K as discharge — and rule the
adjacency principle knowingly, with its scope.** Load-bearing reason: the decomposition
is genuinely decision-free (`id` trivially; `mix` is a total convex-combination
combinator whose one slot is grid-priced; `step` reduces to the adjacency question) —
*provided* adjacency is the covering relation of a canonical order. Rule it now:
**adjacency = the covering relation of the ordered vocabulary's canonical order (forced,
as root-½ was); extending the walk decomposition to an unordered carrier reopens
adjacency as a declared residue, not a free inheritance.** Stating the scope at K is
cheap; discovering a silent graph-choice smuggled into a generative frontier three
boundaries later is not. `mix` itself is clean; its residual softness is only that its
ρ-slot inherits an *unforced coordinate* (A6, rides on K) — the same open-coordinate
question as tau, not a decision hidden in `mix`.

## R4 — The guard's binding arithmetic

**(a) What YES commits to.** The truncation-overconfidence guard assigns pessimism
**per frontier interval**: for each interval, mass = its subtree Kraft sum, likelihood
≤ its sup (closed form for Bernoulli), utility = the interval **endpoint minimum**
(E[u|θ] linear in θ for binary outcomes ⇒ the interval min is an endpoint). This is
stated as **law**, and it *excludes* a single global worst-case utility on the whole
remainder.

**(b) What it forecloses.** The global reading. Executed, the global reading blocks
`respond` forever at every purchase depth (the unowned upper tail always holds θ→1 at
sup-likelihood 1) — the incumbent latent@1 constant-block failure rebuilt *as law*, an
unpriced throttle in the fatal direction. Pinning per-interval as law rules that out
permanently rather than discouraging it.

**(c) Strongest case to reject/amend.** Two faithful readings of the pre-amendment
sentence diverged between "releases at the stakes threshold" and "blocks forever" — so
the amendment is necessary. The residual risk is the opposite: pinning *endpoint
minimum* as law could let a future **non-binary-outcome family** inherit endpoint
evaluation *silently*, where E[u|·] is not linear and the true interval minimum is
interior — under-blocking in the fatal direction. The reviewer's D4 "flips me" is
exactly this and is marked out of scope for the Bernoulli instance R-1 ships.

**(d) Recommendation.** **Endorse (a) and (b), with a one-line wording fix that makes
(b) structural rather than a footnote.** The law is the **interval *minimum* of the
family's E[u]** (never a global worst-case); the **endpoint evaluation is the *Bernoulli
instance's computation* of that minimum**, licensed by binary-outcome linearity. Word
it in that order. Load-bearing reason: this simultaneously (i) rules out the global
reading forever and (ii) keeps the generalization *light* — a non-binary family then
reopens the *instance computation* (recompute the interval minimum, possibly interior)
under the same per-interval criterion, exactly as change-point families enter §3's
carried-statistic criterion without constitutional amendment. The current §3 sentence
leads with "endpoint minimum" and derives it from linearity; a future reader grepping
the guard rule sees the wrong headline. Invert the emphasis at the same freeze.

## R5 — What "byte-identical" now means

**(a) What YES commits to.** The coherence theorem's byte-identity is re-scoped to
**purchase-order independence through one canonical scorer** (posterior a pure function
of `(dl, n1, n0)` per node, owned set folded in canonical code order, one log-sum-exp).
Scorer-vs-incremental agreement is at **tolerance (1e-9), never byte** — because the
live `cond` path (n1 successive additions + per-tick normalization) *cannot* be
IEEE-754-equal to the scorer's `n1·log θ`. The residue tag now covers the **arithmetic
seam**, not just refine-availability. The theorem's constitutional force — the mutation
tripwire reds when a forgetting factor is injected into the carried stats — is
preserved because it reds against the **scorer** fixture (i), not the tolerance fixture.

**(b) What it forecloses.** "Byte-identical against the live incremental path" — IEEE
754 makes that impossible, and pretending otherwise would red the property test for
floating-point-ordering reasons unrelated to the theorem.

**(c) Strongest case to reject/amend — the relocation the convergence smoothed.** The
re-scoping quietly moves the constitutional property from *"the agent does not forget"*
to *"the scorer is self-consistent."* Those coincide **only if the live agent is the
scorer.** The plan routes exchangeable live posteriors through the scorer as its
"cleanest reading" — i.e. **conditionally, not as a commitment.** If R keeps a separate
live incremental path, fixture (i) polices the scorer's self-consistency while a
forgetting factor injected into the *live* path is caught only at tolerance (ii), where
a small factor can hide under 1e-9. The tripwire would then police a shadow.

**(d) Recommendation.** **Accept the re-scoping — and convert "cleanest reading" into a
*ruling*: R routes exchangeable live posteriors through the canonical scorer.**
Load-bearing reason: the mutation tripwire only polices what the scorer computes; make
the scorer what the agent actually runs, or the strongest available anti-smuggling
property guards a path the agent doesn't take. This is also the single-reasoner spirit
of brief §10.1/§10.3 (one canonical numeric path behind the accessor, not two
associativities drifting). The plan flags a per-tick perf envelope for the
fold-the-owned-set-each-tick cost (§9.2, measure-before-ruling); if that envelope
forbids routing-through, *that* reopens the question as a measured perf/constitution
tradeoff for you — but the **default must be the constitutionally stronger single
scorer**, not a shadow oracle checked at tolerance.
